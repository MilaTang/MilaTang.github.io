something about the Spring Festival

2021-02-09

###ORIGIN
The origin of the Spring Festival noe is too old to be traced,the word nian,which in modern Chinese solely means ‘year’,was originally the name of a monster.

The term ‘Guo nian’,which may mean ‘Survive the Nian’,becomes today’s ‘Celebrate the New Year’.

###TIME
This festival is from the first day to the 15th day of the first lunar month.

###CUSTOMS
1.On the arrival of New Year,every family will paste couplets

2.Family members that have not seen each other for a long time will get together for Family Reunion dinner.

3.Stay up late on New Year’s Eve

4.Hand out red packets

5.We need to bring some gifts along fruits,cream cakes.Medical tonics are often for parents and aged relatives,and cash enclosed ina red envelop are for the kids.

6.Set off fireworks

The habit does bring kids joy and excitement as long as precautions are taken.
Fantastic,the whole city is echoing with cracking sounds.
SPECIAL FOOD
rice cake /nian gao/jiao zi/dumpling/sweet dumplings/tang yuan
The first day,we southerners hanve sweet rice cakes for breakfast,and people in the north have jiaozi.
